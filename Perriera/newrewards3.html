Connecticut Science Center STEM Achievement Awards

Each year, the Connecticut Science Center celebrates the achievements of professionals, groups, advocates, businesses, students, and leaders who share our mission to inspire lifelong learning through interactive and innovative experiences that explore our changing world through science. Winners will be selected by the 2022 Gala Committee based on overall contributions to STEM in the region, opening STEM Careers to diverse audiences, and impacts on STEM Education in the community.

Awards will be presented at the Connecticut Science Center Gala: Back to the Future on Saturday, October 1, 2022 at the Connecticut Convention Center.

The STEM Achievement Awards are presented by Stanley Black & Decker.

PETIT FAMILY FOUNDATION WOMEN IN SCIENCE LEADERSHIP AWARDS

These annual awards recognize a woman and young girl working in STEM who are leaders in their fields, and who makes a significant effort to support other women and encourage girls' interests in STEM. The honoree is selected by the Connecticut Science Center's Women in Science steering committee

Awards will be presented at the Connecticut Science Center Gala: Back to the Future on Saturday, October 1, 2022 at the Connecticut Convention Center.

The STEM Achievement Awards are presented by Stanley Black & Decker.
